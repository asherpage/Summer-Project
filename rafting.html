Salt Water Rafting
clear_night

June, 30th

Me and 3 of my friends went salt water tubing so we left at 8 in the morning and drove up to where the buses take you to the top of the river. Once we got on the river it was really nice and relaxing and when we were on the river for a bit we ran into some spots with really strong currents and we were running into rocks and trees pretty frequently.

June, 30th

Eventually we made it to the cliff jumping spot which we stayed at for about an hour. It was fun but really exhausting because after jumping off the cliff the current was really strong so you would have to swim as fast as you could in the other direction. Overall, salt water tubing was really fun.
